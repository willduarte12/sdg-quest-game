Gender equality — timeline challenge
Inventions and discoveries by women throughout history

Throughout history, women have invented and discovered things that changed everyday life — yet many of their names were left out of textbooks, and credit was often given to men.

From the dishwasher to the chocolate chip cookie, from the windshield wiper to Monopoly — women created products and solutions that billions of people use every single day.

SDG 5 — Gender Equality recognizes that true equality means acknowledging women's contributions in every field — including science, technology, and invention. Knowing these names is part of building a more equal world.
